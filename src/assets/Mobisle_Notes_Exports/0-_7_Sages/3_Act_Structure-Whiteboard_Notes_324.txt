Pages 1 to 10 we need the hero and the world he or she or they inhabit / live in.
Page 10 the inciting incident Dash something out of the ordinary that happens to our heroes. 
Pages 11- 25
Denying the call – filler info the plot thickens. How it is our hero or Heroes deny the call?
Page 25
Our hero is lost into the story they make a commitment / involvement / take the red pill - make the choice
Pages 26-45
Cages 45-50 things get worse circumstances change, bad news appears, added pressure to succeed. Foreshadowing, failure, attempts, prove something to himself and others.
Pages 51 - 66 something even worse happens.
Pages 66-80. Things get as bad as they possibly can and all hope is lost – agents have Morpheus Neo has to choose to save Morpheus or let him die in The Matrix.
Pages 81 - 91 now I show the audience that winning against the antagonist is going to be even harder than we thought it was going to be. Neo fights agent Smith and is no match for him because he's much more powerful in The Matrix.
Pages 90-100 the climax your hero wins - hooray! Neo kicks mrs. Ass, dies, comes back to life hoorah! Luke shoots Down DeathStar down Han Solo comes back and helps him escape. 
Pages 100 - 110 Our Hero Walks off into the sunset, bond gets laid and show us the bad guys and Thorns the plan to destroy the world. The world has been changed for the better by our hero in some way?
Bad guy dies or lives injured in some way for the sequel It's A New Beginning Neil flies off into the sunset he is the one. Luke saves the Galaxy from utter destruction from the Sith and destroys Darth Vader his father.
